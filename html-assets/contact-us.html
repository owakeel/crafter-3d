Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What's your warranty policy?

All Bambu Lab printers come with a 12-month warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available for commercial users.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days in the US, 7-14 days internationally. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for educational institutions and students. Contact our sales team with your educational credentials.

Can I use third-party filaments?

Absolutely! While we recommend Bambu Lab filaments for optimal performance, our printers work with most third-party PLA, PETG, and ABS filaments.
